Rabindranath Tagore

Rabindranath Tagore (1861–1941), the luminous polymath from Calcutta, India, left an indelible mark on literature, art, and education. Born into a distinguished family, with his father Debendranath Tagore and mother Sarada Devi, Rabindranath's early poetic brilliance blossomed into a prolific literary career. At 16, he published his first collection, foreshadowing a lifetime of creative exploration.
