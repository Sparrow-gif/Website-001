ticket to ride

"Ticket to Ride" is a popular board game designed by Alan R. Moon. Published in 2004 by Days of Wonder, it has since become a classic in the board game world. In the game, players collect train cards of various colors and use them to claim railway routes on a map, aiming to connect cities and complete specific routes depicted on their "destination tickets." Points are earned for completing routes, and bonus points are awarded for connecting distant cities or completing tickets. The game combines strategy, planning, and a bit of luck, making it enjoyable for players of all ages and levels of experience. It has spawned numerous expansions and editions set in various regions around the world.
