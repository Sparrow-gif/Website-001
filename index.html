Krishna Quotes: Best Lord Krishna Quotes From Srimad Bhagavad Gita

Updated on : nov 22, 2023

❮ ❯

Sri Krishna's quotes hold immense value in daily life, serving as guiding beacons for navigating life's complexities. His teachings, found in the Bhagavad Gita, resonate across ages, offering wisdom on duty, righteousness, and the path to inner peace. Embracing concepts like selflessness, devotion, and detached action, his words inspire a balanced approach to challenges, encouraging one to fulfill responsibilities with unwavering dedication while surrendering the fruits of actions. Applying these teachings nurtures a resilient mindset, fostering harmony, compassion, and spiritual growth amidst life's trials and triumphs. Integrating Sri Krishna's profound guidance illuminates a path towards a purposeful and fulfilling existence.

"You have a right to perform your prescribed duties, but you are not entitled to the fruits of your actions."

-Lord krishna

"आपको अपने निर्धारित कर्तव्यों को पूरा करने का अधिकार है, लेकिन आप अपने कार्यों के फल के हकदार नहीं हैं।"

-भगवान कृष्ण

"Do everything you have to do, but not with greed, not with ego, not with lust, not with envy but with love, compassion, humility, and devotion."

-Lord krishna

"तुम्हें जो कुछ करना है वह करो, लेकिन लालच से नहीं, अहंकार से नहीं, वासना से नहीं, ईर्ष्या से नहीं बल्कि प्रेम, करुणा, विनम्रता और भक्ति के साथ।"

-भगवान कृष्ण

"The self-controlled soul, who moves amongst sense objects, free from either attachment or repulsion, he wins eternal peace."

-Lord krishna

"आत्म-नियंत्रित आत्मा, जो इंद्रिय विषयों के बीच आसक्ति या विकर्षण से मुक्त होकर विचरण करती है, वह शाश्वत शांति प्राप्त करती है।"

-भगवान कृष्ण

"Whatever happened, happened for the good. Whatever is happening, is happening for the good. Whatever will happen, will also happen for the good."

-Lord krishna

"जो हुआ, अच्छे के लिए हुआ। जो हो रहा है, अच्छे के लिए हो रहा है। जो होगा, वह भी अच्छे के लिए होगा।"

-भगवान कृष्ण

"The soul is not born, it never dies; nor once in existence, it never ceases. The soul is birthless, eternal, immortal and ageless."

-Lord krishna

"আত্মা জন্মগ্রহণ করে না, এটি কখনও মরে না; বা একবার অস্তিত্বে থাকলে, এটি কখনও শেষ হয় না। আত্মা জন্মহীন, চিরন্তন, অমর এবং অকাল।"

-भगवान कृष्ण
